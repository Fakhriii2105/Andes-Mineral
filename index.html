About Andes Mineral Corp

Andes Mineral Corp is a resource development company focused on mineral exploration, sustainable mining, and energy innovation. With operations inspired by the rich geology of the Andes region, we combine modern engineering with responsible extraction practices to deliver high-value resources for global industry.

Our mission is to operate safely, efficiently, and ethically — ensuring long-term benefits for our stakeholders, communities, and the environment.
